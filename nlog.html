LOL:Wild Rift VS Vivo S1

My Gameplay in League of Legends: Wild Rift using my phone Vivo S1.

League of Legends
Wild Rift

LoL: WR is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Riot Games for mobile devices and consoles. It is a streamlined version of the popular PC game League of Legends (LoL), designed to provide a similar experience with controls and mechanics optimized for mobile and console gameplay.

My Interface of Menu and Profile

In League of Legends: Wild Rift, the interface of the menu and profile is designed to be intuitive and visually appealing, providing quick access to game modes, events, and player customization. The main menu serves as the central hub with easily navigable tabs for your profile, store, events, friends, and settings. The profile section prominently displays your summoner icon, level, rank, and detailed statistics, along with options to customize your champion skins and loadouts. Notifications and alerts are clearly visible, ensuring you stay updated on new messages, friend requests, and game invites, all while maintaining the epic fantasy aesthetic that aligns with the game's overall theme.

LOL: WR (Map,Rank icons,Spell icons)

"League of Legends: Wild Rift" features a streamlined map called the Wild Rift, designed for shorter, fast-paced matches on mobile devices. This map, a smaller version of the classic Summoner's Rift, retains three lanes and jungle areas with key objectives like the Baron Nashor and Dragons. The rank icons in Wild Rift reflect the competitive tiers players can achieve, ranging from Iron to Challenger, each with distinct, visually appealing symbols that signify a playerâ€™s skill level. Additionally, the spell icons are designed to be clear and easily recognizable, ensuring quick access and efficient use during intense gameplay moments.

Vivo S1

This is My Phone, Vivo S1, released in 2019, is a stylish mid-range smartphone featuring a 6.38-inch Super AMOLED display with a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels. Powered by a MediaTek Helio P65 processor, it comes with options of 4GB or 6GB of RAM and 64GB or 128GB of internal storage. The device boasts a triple rear camera setup with a 16MP main sensor, an 8MP ultra-wide lens, and a 2MP depth sensor, alongside a 32MP front camera for high-quality selfies. Running on Funtouch OS 9 based on Android 9 Pie, it includes an in-display fingerprint sensor and a robust 4500mAh battery with 18W fast charging. Its sleek design, dual SIM support, and gradient color options make the Vivo S1 an attractive and practical choice for users. This is what Ive been using to Play LOL: Wild Rift, eventhough sometimes I've encounter that my phone began to throttle the phone still satisfies me.

My Victories

Securing victories in "League of Legends: Wild Rift" has been an exhilarating experience that highlights my strategic prowess and teamwork as a Support/AD Carry. Climbing to Gold rank, I've demonstrated a solid understanding of game mechanics and the ability to adapt swiftly to changing scenarios. My role as the primary shotcaller has been pivotal, leading my team to coordinated attacks and defenses that turned the tide in crucial matches. As a support, my strategic ward placements and timely crowd control have been crucial, while as an AD Carry, my consistent damage output and positioning have often secured team fights. These accomplishments reflect my dedication and continuous growth in mastering Wild Rift.
